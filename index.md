## 2. Emphasis  

- *Italic text* with `*asterisks*` or `_underscores_`  
- **Bold text** with `**double asterisks**` or `__underscores__`  
- ***Bold and italic*** with triple asterisks  
- ~~Strikethrough~~ with `~~tildes~~`
